When entering a game, have the players decide who will
be Jason and who will be the camp counselors.
 
Make sure to type "set cheats 1" (without quoatations) 
into the command BEFORE starting your game!

TO PLAY AS JASON: type in command "give invulnerability"
TO PLAY AS COUNSELOR: type in command "give silencer"

Goal of the Game: 
If Jason: kill all the counselors!
If Counselor: kill Jason!
